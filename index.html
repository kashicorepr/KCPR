Who we are

A PR agency rooted in Kashi, built for growth.

KASHI CORE PR is a talent & PR agency working out of Varanasi. We sit between local and regional businesses and the creators who already have their audience's trust — and we make the introduction count.

We handle the parts that usually slow a collaboration down: matching the right creator to the right brand, agreeing terms (paid, barter, or hybrid), scheduling the shoot, and making sure both sides walk away with something worth doing again.

For businesses, that means real reach without guesswork. For creators, it means steady, well-briefed work instead of chasing brands in your DMs.
